Local Walks

Explore some of our favourite walking spots in and around Marlborough.

Savernake Forest

Easy · Woodland · Peaceful

Ancient woodland just outside Marlborough, ideal for a relaxed stroll surrounded by some of England’s oldest trees.

The Ridgeway

Moderate · Views · Countryside

A scenic route with beautiful views across the Wiltshire Downs and a great option for guests looking for a classic countryside walk.

Avebury

Easy · Historic · Scenic

A memorable place to explore with a unique prehistoric setting and lovely surrounding countryside.

Hackpen White Horse

Moderate · Hillside · Views

A scenic Wiltshire walk with open countryside and panoramic views across the surrounding landscape.
